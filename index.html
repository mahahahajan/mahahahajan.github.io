About

I am a year old coder who loves computers. I started programming at the age of 8 and I haven't stopped since. I teach coding to students and mentor at the local coderdojo. When I'm not coding I'm off playing Pokemon Go (and other videogames), biking, hanging with friends, or reading. I have won:
